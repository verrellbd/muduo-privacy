# Mu Duo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026

Mu Duo ("the App") is developed by Delapan Prima Developer ("we", "us").
This policy explains how the App handles your information.

## 1. No Data Collection
Mu Duo does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information
on remote servers. The App operates entirely offline.

## 2. Account & Sign-in
The App does not require an account or sign-in to use any feature.

## 3. Local Storage
The following data is stored only on your device:
- Bookmarks and likes you create on scripture passages
- Reading progress
- App settings (theme, language, font)
- Daily streak count
- Holy day reminder preferences

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App permanently
deletes all local data.

## 4. Notifications
The App may schedule local notifications on your device for holy day
reminders. These are generated and delivered entirely by your device.
No notification data is sent to any server.

## 5. External Links
The App may open external services (e.g., Google Maps) when you tap
"Open in Maps" on a Lithang location. These services have their own
privacy policies which apply once you leave the App.

## 6. Children's Privacy
The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any
information from children.

## 7. Changes to this Policy
We may update this policy. Updates will be posted at this URL with
a revised "Last updated" date.
